GDPR Compliance

1. Introduction

At OpenGates Insurance, we are committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

2. Data We Collect

We may collect personal data including your name, email, phone number, policy details, and transaction history to provide and improve our services.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on:

  • Contractual necessity for providing insurance services.
  • Legal obligations under regulatory requirements.
  • Your explicit consent where applicable.
  • Legitimate interests for service improvement and fraud prevention.

4. Your Rights Under GDPR

  • Right to access your personal data.
  • Right to rectify inaccurate information.
  • Right to request erasure ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to restrict or object to processing.
  • Right to data portability.
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law.

6. Data Security

We implement strong technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

7. International Transfers

Where applicable, we ensure that any transfer of your personal data outside the European Economic Area (EEA) is protected by appropriate safeguards.
