Cookie Policy

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a smoother experience by remembering preferences, improving performance, and delivering relevant content.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for core functionality such as login and navigation.
  • Performance Cookies: Collect information on site usage to help us improve performance.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember preferences such as theme settings or language.
  • Advertising Cookies: Used to deliver personalized ads and track their effectiveness.

3. How We Use Cookies

OpenGates Insurance uses cookies to provide seamless navigation, enhance security, analyze traffic, and offer customized content and services tailored to your needs.

4. Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling essential cookies may impact website functionality.

5. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services (such as analytics providers) that place cookies on your device to help analyze site usage and improve service delivery.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
