Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

At OpenGates Insurance, we are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you interact with our services.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal details such as name, email, phone number, and address.
  • Account information used for registration and login.
  • Insurance-related details for policy processing.
  • Technical data like IP address, browser type, and device info.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and manage insurance services.
  • To improve our website and client portal functionality.
  • To communicate updates, policies, and support information.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

4. Data Protection

We implement strong security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. While no method is 100% secure, we continually update our practices to safeguard your privacy.

5. Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request corrections to inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request deletion of your data where applicable.
  • Opt out of marketing communications.
