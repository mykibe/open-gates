Documentation

Welcome to the official documentation for OpenGates. Here you will find guides, references, and tutorials to help you make the most out of the system.

Quick Start

  1. Register as an agent or client via the signup page.
  2. Verify your account through the email confirmation.
  3. Log in to access your dashboard and available services.
  4. Manage policies, payments, and reports with ease.

Core Features

  • Policy Management: Create, update, and track insurance policies.
  • Client Portal: Allow customers to view policies, claims, and invoices.
  • Agent Dashboard: Monitor performance and manage client requests.
  • Reports & Analytics: Generate detailed reports with charts and insights.

API Documentation

Our API allows integration with third-party services. Below is a quick reference: 

POST /api/v1/login
GET  /api/v1/policies
POST /api/v1/claims
GET  /api/v1/reports

FAQ

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the email instructions.

Can clients view invoices online?

Yes, clients can log in to their portal and download invoices anytime.

Does the system support mobile devices?

Yes, the platform is fully responsive and works seamlessly on mobile.
